Our products are fully reusable and recyclable as certified PS 6 which is the best recycling seal you have.


Some products can be considered sustainable because they generate less losses, because they are recyclable or more durable. Others because they contain fewer harmful or toxic substances, or because their production process consumes less energy. Products that generate income for cooperatives, artisans or low-income communities are also considered sustainable.


The sustainable product is the one that presents the best environmental performance throughout its life cycle, with function, quality and level of satisfaction equal to, or better, compared to a standard product.
